❓ Troubleshooting Common Issues

Installation blocked by Windows

Click “More info” and then “Run anyway”. Mullvad is a trusted application with no malware.

VPN does not connect

Check your internet connection. Restart the app. If the problem continues, try a different server from the list.

Slow internet speed

VPNs can slow your speed a little. Choose a server close to your location for better performance. The “Auto” option usually works best.

I forgot my account number

Mullvad gives you a 16‑digit account number when you subscribe. You can find it in the app under “Account”. If you lose it, contact Mullvad support with your payment details.
